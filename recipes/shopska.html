Shopska Salad

The Shopska salad is the most famous salad in Bulgaria

It's really quick and easy to make, but at the same time is one of the tastiest snacks you can have if you're in a rush.

The salad itself also has some patriotic roots, as it's ingredients make up the colors of the Bulgarian Flag.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cucumbers
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • OPTIONAL: Olives, Olive Oil, Vinegar, Salt, Pepper

Steps

  1. Dice the tomatoes and cucumbers
  2. Place the diced contents in a bowl, and stir for 30 seconds
  3. Add a small amount of salt, pepper, and olive oil
  4. Grate cheese over the contents
  5. Add olives, and a little more olive oil
