Better* Instant Ramen Noodles

Learn how to spice up your instant noodles and make them better

Ramen Noodles aren't exactly the most nutritious meal.

However, there are times where a pack of noodles and a few other ingredients is all you have in kitchen.

You'd be surprised how easy it is to make them much better by simply adding a few extra things on top.

Ingredients

  • A packet of Ramen Noodles
  • Chickpeas (canned, or in a jar)
  • Fresh Herbs (Any that you have)
  • Salt, Pepper, Paprika
  • A lime, or a lemon, or lemon juice

Steps

  1. Heat 500ml of water in a Kettle or in your Microwave
  2. While the water is heating up, place the packet of Ramen Noodles in a bowl, and squeeze your lime over it. Flip over, and do the other side as well.
  3. Add your salt, pepper, and paprika on both sides. Avoid using the small packet that comes with your noodles.
  4. Take your chickpeas, and drain them of all water. Once done, place them around your noodles.
  5. Since your water should be done by now, pour it slowly and evenly inside the bowl.
  6. Finally, take any fresh or dried herbs you may have lying around, and dumb them on top.
  7. Cover your bowl with a dish for 3-5 minutes.
