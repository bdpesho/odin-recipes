Tarator

There is nothing better than Tarator on a hot summer's day

As with everything else so far, this dish is easy and quick to make.

The Tarator is available in every single restaurant in Bulgaria, and is widely regarded as an excellent dish during summer.

It's also very light and low-calorie meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cucumbers
  • 500 grams of Bulgarian Kiselo Mlyako, or Greek Yogurk
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • 1 Clove of Garlic
  • Fennel
  • 500ml cold water

Steps

  1. Peel the cucumbers and cut into small cubes. Alternatively, you can grate them
  2. Stir the kiselo mlyako while still in the pot, then add it to the cucumbers and continue stirring.
  3. Pour in half a liter of cold water (more or less water may be needed depending on the desired consistency).
  4. Crush the garlic with some salt in a mortar and pestle, then add to the soup together with ground walnuts and finely chopped dill.
  5. Cut the fennel into small thin pieces, and drop into the soup. Finally, top up with a small amount of olive oil.
